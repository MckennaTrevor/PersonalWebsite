How to Keep Bees

Hive Checks

The most important thing to do when keeping bees is making sure they are healthy and comfortable. To make sure those thigns are true, you need to check the hive. In these checks, you want to make sure that the queen is laying eggs, there are no illnesses in the hive, and that the bees have space.

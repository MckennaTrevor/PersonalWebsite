History of Beekeeping

Beekeeping started as foragers going out and taking the honey from natural wild colonies of bees. In a cave in spain dating back to 9000 BC, there is a painting depcting someone taking honey from a hive. beekeeping how we know it started around 2500 BC, in Egypt, and likely even earlier in china.

Honey seeker depicted on 8000 year old cave painting at Arana Caves in Spain.



How to Keep Bees

Keeping bees can be a very rewarding and fun hobby. To properly care for your bees, you need to make sure they are healthy, properly reproducing, and are comfortable. To ensure these things are true, you need to check your hive occasionally. Every check, you should look for eggs and larvae to make sure the queen is good, clean out any extra wax, check for illness, and add more space if needed.

Honey

There are three main types of honey

  • liquid honey
  • chunk honey
  • whipped honey

Each honey had a different flavour and texture. These arent the only types, but they are the most common. Liquid honey is the best if you want the raw taste of honey, or if you want to drizzel it over something. Chunk honey can really trell you the personality of the hive, as it has a square of honey comb floating in it, adding new flavor. whip0ped honey has undergone controlled sugar crystalyzation and airation, leaving it white and creamy.
